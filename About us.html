Let us introduce ourselves in every detail!
The company called "Your Coffee" was established about six months ago in a small house in Stockholm. It was a small company which had neither offices nor large warehouses as it happens to be. We are a married couple named Åsa Peterson and Christian Andersson.We were never satisfied with the coffee we drank every morning and for that we were inspired to dedicate ourselves to it.A good morning always starts with a good coffee. So the idea to open a company with a main purpose was born to bring a new standard to market.We initially started trading only to our friends and family until the work went on with a satisfactory development.
In addition to coffee,we saw as a good opportunity the marketing of coffee machines.This for making the customer satisfacted.Currently we work with ten talented and highly skilled workers who have helped us to move forward in six months and now have our offices and very good connections with other companies. Every day is valuable for us so we try to every day more and more to raise our company. Help us set a new standard for coffee which is the first love every morning.
We welcome you to our events and we look forward on collaborating with any of you!
Åsa Peterson and Christian Andersson!
